About PrimeGate

PrimeGate delivers trusted infrastructure for tokenizing, distributing, and servicing institutional-grade real-world assets. Learn how our team is building compliant on-chain pathways for asset managers, banks, and treasury leaders.

Building the infrastructure for compliant tokenized finance

PrimeGate orchestrates the full lifecycle of institutional real-world assets—from structuring and tokenization through multi-jurisdictional distribution and servicing. Our team blends capital markets expertise with deep Web3 engineering to make regulated assets programmable.

We partner with asset managers, banks, and corporate treasuries to unlock new liquidity while preserving governance, risk controls, and investor protections in every market we operate.

Our mission and vision

PrimeGate exists to bridge traditional finance and decentralized infrastructure. We deliver institutional confidence to tokenized assets without compromising on compliance, governance, or investor protections.

  • Our Mission
  • Our Vision

Our mission is to make regulated real-world assets accessible on-chain by unifying issuance, investor onboarding, settlement, and lifecycle servicing in one secure operating system.

  • Institutional-grade KYC, AML, and compliance controls
  • Integrated reporting and governance for issuers and investors

Our vision is an open capital market where every qualified institution can launch and invest in fully programmable asset products with instant distribution across global networks.

  • Interoperable architecture that scales with evolving token standards
