TOKENIZATION WORKBENCH

Configure once. Offer everywhere you’re authorized.

Eligibility rules

(Jurisdictions, Investor Types)

PrimeGate allows issuers to set precise geographic and investor-type restrictions—automatically validating jurisdictional eligibility and investor classification (institutional, accredited, or retail) before any subscription or transfer occurs.

Transfer restrictions & lockups

Configure lockup periods, wallet-to-wallet transfer limits, and KYC re-checks to ensure every transaction remains compliant. Transfer rules are enforced on-chain, providing full visibility and auditability for issuers and regulators.

NAV & pricing oracles integration

(if applicable)

PrimeGate integrates with trusted data oracles to update Net Asset Value (NAV) and market prices in real time—giving both managers and investors accurate, tamper-resistant pricing data across the asset lifecycle.

Corporate actions

(Subscriptions, Redemptions, Distributions)

Manage investor subscriptions, redemptions, and distributions digitally—with built-in validation, notifications, and immutable audit logs that reduce manual workload and operational errors.
